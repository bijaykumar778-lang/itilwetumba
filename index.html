About the Institute

Government Industrial Training Institute (LWE), Tumba is a state government technical institute dedicated to skill development in Left Wing Extremism affected regions of Bihar. Government Schemes Implemented through ITI

Government ITI (LWE), Tumba actively supports and implements various flagship Government of India schemes aimed at skill development, employment generation and self-reliance. These schemes provide additional learning, certification and livelihood opportunities to ITI trainees.

Kaushal Yuva Program (KYP)

Kaushal Yuva Program is a Bihar Government initiative focused on enhancing employability skills of youth through basic computer literacy, communication skills and personality development. ITI trainees benefit by strengthening their foundational skills alongside technical training.

Implementing Agency: Government of Bihar

Pradhan Mantri Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana

This flagship scheme aims to provide rooftop solar installations and up to 300 units of free electricity per month to households. ITI trainees, especially from Electrician and related trades, gain opportunities in solar installation, maintenance and green energy employment.

Ministry: Ministry of New & Renewable Energy, Government of India

Pradhan Mantri Vishwakarma Yojana

PM Vishwakarma Yojana supports traditional artisans and skilled workers through training, modern tools, certification and financial assistance. ITI trainees are encouraged to upgrade their skills and explore self-employment and entrepreneurship opportunities under this scheme.

Ministry: Ministry of MSME, Government of India

Dual System of Training (DST)

Under the Dual System of Training, trainees receive institutional training at ITI along with on-the-job practical training in industries. This system bridges the gap between classroom learning and real industrial work environment, enhancing employability.

Academic Authority: Directorate General of Training (DGT)

Participation in these schemes is subject to eligibility, government guidelines and availability of programs. Students are advised to contact the institute administration for detailed information and enrollment procedures.
